Science

Science encompasses the study of the natural world, including the physical, biological, and chemical aspects.

It involves observation, experimentation, and analysis to understand how things work and to discover new knowledge.

Technology

Technology refers to the practical application of scientific knowledge for various purposes.

It involves the development, implementation, and use of tools, machines, systems, and processes to solve problems, improve efficiency, and enhance human capabilities.

Engineering

Engineering involves the application of scientific and mathematical principles to design, create, and improve structures, machines, systems, and processes.

Engineers use their knowledge and skills to innovate, solve problems, and contribute to the development of new technologies.

Mathematics

Mathematics is the study of numbers, quantities, shapes, and patterns.

It provides a language and tools for understanding and describing the world around us, as well as for solving complex problems. Mathematics plays a crucial role in various scientific and technological fields.

Why choose STEM STRAND?

  • Curiosity
  • Innovation
  • Impact

Curiosity

STEM education encourages curiosity and nurtures the natural inquisitiveness of students. It promotes hands-on learning, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, enabling students to actively engage in the learning process. STEM education also fosters creativity and innovation, encouraging students to think outside the box and find unique solutions.

Innovation

STEM education prepares students for the future by equipping them with essential skills such as adaptability, resilience, and collaboration. It helps develop a growth mindset and the ability to embrace and learn from failure. Students who engage in STEM education are more likely to pursue STEM-related careers, which are in high demand and offer competitive salaries.

Impact

STEM education bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications. It emphasizes the integration of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, providing a holistic understanding of how these subjects intersect and apply to everyday life. STEM education also promotes digital literacy and prepares students to navigate and succeed in a technology-driven world.

Different fields under STEM strand

Natural, physical, and life sciences (sometimes including medicine)

Computer, electronics, and other technology-related disciplines

All types of engineering

Mathematics, or any field involving a heavy application of mathematical principles
